Two Versions One Great Product

Windcatcher is designed to find solution to any scenario life throws at you, that is why we design two different versions. We've taken the best of the ideas from flying turbines and have created a hybrid between a flying system and a more traditional wind turbine. By doing so, we've reduced the weight of materials by almost 90%. No more big poles supported by concrete or lifting cranes!

Wind Catcher Lite (200W Version)

The 200W version is the ideal turbine for lower wind speed climates. Full power is reached already at a 5.5 m/s (12 mph) wind speed. Ideal for running a small fridge, a few lights, your laptop and router. Fits with smaller portable power stations of 150-600 Wh battery capacity.

  • 10kg / 22lbs weight
  • (up to) 200W output
  • 24V power

Wind Catcher (600W Version)

The 600W can in windy areas deliver enough power for personal electric equipment, charge e-bikes or power tools or slow boil water. It works well with larger power stations with battery capacity in the range of 600 Wh to 2400 Wh. The turbine reaches full power at a wind speed of 7.5 m/s (17 mph).

  • 10kg / 22lbs weight
  • (up to) 600W output
  • 48V power
